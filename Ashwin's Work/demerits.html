Merits and Demerits Of Web 3.0

Web 3.0 or the third version of the internet is expected to bring a paradigm shift in the way we browse, interact socially, or even shop on the internet. While Web 2.0 improved communication and online interaction, Web 3.0 will focus on improving personalisation, collective ownership, and sharing of content. The updated version of the internet will make use of blockchain (the technology underlying bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies) and hence, allow the internet to be supported by decentralised networks. As a result, website owners will no longer need to depend on servers owned by big companies.
